Frequently Asked Questions

What is cellular meat?

Cellular meat is real meat grown from animal cells, offering a sustainable and ethical alternative to traditional livestock farming.

Is cellular meat safe to eat?

Yes, cellular meat undergoes rigorous testing and meets all safety and quality standards before reaching your plate.

How does cellular meat help the environment?

It significantly reduces greenhouse gas emissions, water usage, and deforestation associated with conventional meat production.
